About me

I am 16 year old African American boy born in Nigeria to two wonderful parents, Adeyemi and Folake. I have an older sister named Favour(18) as well as a younger one named Faith(13) making me the middle child .We moved to the United States when i was about one and since then we have lived in Arizona. I am very thankful for the sacrfices they have made so that i may grow up here, because of them I am very fluent in Math, English, Science and can hold my own in History. They raised me to be as open minded and kind as the situation allows. Thanks to these learned traits I am generally very calm and attentive. I have a passion for sport although I dont have a passion for watching them. The thrill of fighting for a team or for a cause has driven me to try my hand at many sports (football, basketball, soccer, baseball, rugby, and lacross), and I must say that each sport has its own unique thrill. I have volunterred for a total of over 100 hours since my freshman year, some of it with Saint Mary's Food BAnk and some with my high schools Key Club. I love the though of helping other it is one of the many things that drive me to be so aproachable and respectable.
